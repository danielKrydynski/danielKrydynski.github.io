About

Hello! I’m Daniel Krydynski, a passionate Web Developer, UI/UX Designer, and Game Developer based in the Greater St. Louis Area. I specialize in building interactive, user-friendly web applications and digital experiences across multiple platforms.

With a strong foundation in both design and development, I thrive on turning ideas into polished products. My approach centers on iterative design and rapid prototyping, allowing me to quickly gather feedback and deliver solutions that are both functional and visually appealing.

I have experience working with a variety of technologies, including Python, JavaScript, Bootstrap, Salesforce, and game engines like Unity. Whether collaborating with teams or working independently, I’m committed to continuous learning and delivering high-quality results.

When I’m not coding or designing, I enjoy exploring new tech trends, creating digital art, and developing indie games.
